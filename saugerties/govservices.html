Parks & Recreation Spotlight

Seamon Park

A beautiful, hilly park in the Northeast corner of the Village of Saugerties . . . Seamon Park is beautifully landscaped throughout the year. It really shines in early October (in 2006, October 1st to be exact), when the Mum Festival features an incredible variety and number of chrysanthemums, as well as interesting art displays, musical entertainment, and food booths.

Assessment Information

The main function of the Assessor's Office is to maintain an equitable assessment roll. The Assessor is available to discuss your assessment and encourages all property owners to review their file.

Taxable status day is March 1st. All exemption applications must be filed by this date.

The date for the 2016 Grievance Day for the Town of Saugerties is May 24, 2016. It will be held at the Senior Center Building on Market Street between the hours of 4 p.m. and 8 p.m.
