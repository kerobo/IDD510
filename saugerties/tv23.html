What is Lighthouse TV23?

Saugertiesâ€™ public access cable channel (Lighthouse TV23) is a vehicle for community communication, education, artistic expression and other noncommercial uses of video on an open, uncensored and equitable basis.

Lighthouse TV23 is a joint Town and Village of Saugerties undertaking. It is funded by both the Town and Village boards, and those boards are advised by the Lighthouse TV23 Committee.

If you are in the Saugerties TWC viewing area and have an event to publicize, send an email with the information you want displayed (30 words or less) to: lighthousetv@saugerties.ny.us

If you have a video of general interest to Saugerties cable subscribers, you can submit it by filling out a Channel Time Request Form and bringing the form and a DVD (with a menu) to the Town Hall building.
