Decks and Docks on the Esopus

Decks and Docks events have become a fun part of the Saugerties busy event schedule. Participants come by kayak, rowboat, auto, bike, and on foot to explore the Esopus Creek and Esopus Bend Nature Preserve.

Visitors are welcomed to share their decks, docks, lawns, and patios with enthusiasm and generous offerings of food and drink. One participant, looking forward to future events, summed it up: ". . . a splendid introduction to the unexpected beauties of some watery corners of Saugerties, and the opportunity to make some fascinating new friends."

Come on out, in June (see the Calendar for specifics/rain date), to show your support for the Esopus Creek Conservancy (ECC). Come by boat, come by car, ride your bike, walk or hike . . . come delight in the creek and the flora, fauna, and neighbors who call it home.

By popular demand, last year's concert creek-side at Esopus Bend Nature Preserve will be repeated for the added enjoyment of "Decks and Docks" visitors.
