Food Pantry

CONTACT

  • Marilyn Richardson

  • (845) 246-6885

HOURS

  • Monday & Thursday

  • 10 AM to 12 PM

  • Tuesday

  • 7 PM to 8 PM

Staffed by over 50 volunteers the Saugerties Area Council of Churches Food Pantry serves up to 500 men, women and children per month from the Saugerties area.

The food given out is donated by individuals, organizations, businesses, and churches in the Saugerties area and purchased from monetary donations also through Hunger Prevention and Nutrition Assistance Program (HPNAP).

Cancer Services Program

FREE Cancer Screenings Available

The Cancer Services Program of Ulster County (located at the American Cancer Society) provides FREE breast, cervical, and colon cancer screenings for UNINSURED, Ulster County residents.

If you are a woman, age 40 and over, or a man age 50 and over, and you do not have health insurance, call us!

You can register for the program over the phone by calling (845) 339-7896, Ext. 23. Remember, early cancer detection saves lives. Call today.
