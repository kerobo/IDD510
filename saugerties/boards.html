The Town Board established the commission by local law in 1972. Its duties are to survey and monitor area natural resources and environmental conditions. Duties:

The Seamon Park Board's mission includes overseeing the existing Trust Fund and all monetary donations made to the Board by numerous community organizations. Funds are allocated to continually maintain the Park, making necessary improvements where needed, keep abreast of all innovative ideas without altering the Park's natural, aesthetic beauty. The Park Board's goal is that of John Seamon, when he deeded the Park to the Village — "a park, a breathing place, open and free at all times to every person".

Terms are 3 years.

Duties:

Lifespring: Saugerties Adult Learning Community is a locally driven, self-supporting program of classes/courses and special events sponsored by the Town of Saugerties. The duties of the Lifespring Board are to plan and oversee the activities of the program and to decide on policy.

Established in 2009 by volunteers from the community, Lifespring annually has more than 160 participating members. It provides opportunities for adults to: challenge and stimulate their minds in new and/or creative ways; open up fresh vistas; discover new talents, skills, and interests; and meet new friends.
