Zoning Board of Appeals Agenda for the November 7, 2016 Meeting

A hearing before the Zoning Board of Appeals will be held on, Monday, November 7, 2016 at 7 p.m. at the Saugerties Senior Citizens Center (207 Market Street). Saugerties, NY.

The meeting will include a continuation of the Verizon/Cellco Partnership appeal related to placing a tower/shelter on the property at 679 Schoolhouse Road, an appeal by Ashley Homes LLC regarding a denial by the Code Enforcement Officer, pertaining to a sign replacement involving an LED motion sign and an RH zoned property requesting an OK to turn one of their floors into 2 apartments.

For a copy of the agenda, click here.

Any questions should be addressed to 845-246-2800 extension 333.
